  <h1 class="post-title">About Taiwan News Alerts</h1>

  <div class="post-content">

Taiwan News Alerts provides curated security intelligence and news coverage focused on the Taiwan Strait region. Our goal is to deliver timely, accurate information about cross-strait developments, military activities, and regional security dynamics.

## What We Cover

- **Military Activity**: PLA air and naval operations, ADIZ incursions, exercises
- **Defense Developments**: Arms sales, military deployments, defense policy
- **Political Developments**: Cross-strait relations, diplomatic events
- **Regional Security**: US, Japan, and allied activities in the Indo-Pacific

We aggregate information from official government sources, reputable news organizations, and open-source intelligence:

- Taipei Times
- South China Morning Post
- Defense News
